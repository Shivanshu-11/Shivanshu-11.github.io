About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Riverside. My research focuses on Distributed Optimization, where I explore strategies for optimizing agents' behaviors in large-scale networks. I combine concepts from control theory, optimization, and machine learning to develop efficient and scalable algorithms. I am supervised by Prof. Fabio Pasqualetti and Prof. Wei Ren.

I completed my MTech in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur in 2022, and I was awarded the Silver Medal for Best Academic Performance in Cyber Physical Systems. Prior to that, I pursued a BTech in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Srinagar, in 2020.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I enjoy playing Indian classical music on the harmonium, playing badminton, and practicing yoga.
