About Me

I am a first year Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Riverside, under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Pasqualetti and Prof. Wei Ren. I am particularly interested in problems involving optimization strategies at the agent level. To this end, I utilize concepts from control theory, optimization, and machine learning.

I completed my MTech in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur in 2022, for which I was awarded Silver medal 2022 (Certificate of Merit) for Best Academic Performance in Cyber Physical Systems, IIT Jodhpur. Prior to that, I did a BTech in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Srinagar in 2020.

When I am not working, you can find me playing Indian classical music on harmonium (beware, sometimes I even try to sing), playing badminton, or practicing yoga.
